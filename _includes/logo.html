Welcome to my Research Homepage! Currently, I am an undergraduate student at USC, where I collaborate actively with students and faculty. Previously, I completed the MIT Summer Research Program under Dr. Luqiao Liu, and prior was an NSF REU Fellow at Penn State under Dr. Paul Lammert. I am particularly interested in theoretical condensed matter physics, mathematics, and quantum matter + information.
